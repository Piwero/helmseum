mediaserver Helm Chart Usage Notes

Thank you for installing the mediaserver chart!

**Namespace:**
- Resources are deployed in the namespace: `{{ .Values.namespace }}`

**Customizing Deployments:**
- Configure containers, resources, probes, affinity, tolerations, and more in `values.yaml`.
- Example:
  containers:
    - name: my-app
      image: my-image:tag
      resources:
        requests:
          cpu: "100m"
          memory: "128Mi"
        limits:
          cpu: "500m"
          memory: "512Mi"
      livenessProbe:
        httpGet:
          path: /healthz
          port: 8080
        initialDelaySeconds: 10
        periodSeconds: 10

**Services:**
- Define multiple services and ports in `svcs` section of `values.yaml`.

**IngressRoute:**
- Configure hosts, entryPoints, middlewares, and TLS in `ingressRoutes` section.

**Storage:**
- Customize PVCs and PVs with accessModes, storageClass, and NFS options.

**Troubleshooting:**
- Check resource status: `kubectl -n {{ .Values.namespace }} get all`
- View logs: `kubectl -n {{ .Values.namespace }} logs <pod>`
- Dry-run manifests: `helm template .`

For more details, see the README and values.yaml.
